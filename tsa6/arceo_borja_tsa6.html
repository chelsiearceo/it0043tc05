What do we know about Inside Out 2?

Inside Out 2 is a box office hit. According to Variety, the film, which premiered in theaters on June 14, is this year's highest-grossing North American film release. It's brought in $285.7 million, surpassing Dune: Part Two's $282 million. The success of the first movie created a built-in audience for the second, says Northeastern associate teaching professor and pop-culture expert Steve Granelli.

By this point, a discerning viewer has put the pieces together: Riley's secret is that she's gay. Or she's bi, or pansexual, or just generally queer. Except, after Joy and the gang escape from the vault, the Deep Dark Secret never appears again in the main narrative. The film presents itself as a coming-of-age tale of Riley Anderson, a young girl learning how to handle her emotions. It tend towards the existential and can help us reevaluate the foundation we have in the Divine.
