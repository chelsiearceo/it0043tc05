ANNOUNCEMENT

BARBIE WORLD WILL BE CLOSED!

It is with a heavy heart that we announce the closure of Barbie World, effective this coming Sunday, May 19, 2024. After several years of serving our community, we have made the difficult decision to close our doors. We want to express our deepest gratitude to all of our loyal customers who have supported us throughout the years.

As a token of appreciation, we will be offering a clearance sale starting tomorrow, with discounts on all remaining inventory. This is your last chance to grab your favorite Barbie dolls, accessories, and merchandise before we bid farewell. We hope to see you one last time as we say goodbye to Barbie World.

Thank you for your understanding and continued support.

Sincerely,
Barbie World Management
